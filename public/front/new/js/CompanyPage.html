Chicago Toy & Game Group (ChiTAG)

  • Website (www.chitag.com) | Employees | Featured in
    •

Founded by Mary Couzin in 2003 ChiTAG Group has a series of events promoting and celebrating play and innovation with events such as: Chicago Toy & Game Fair, TAGIEs, Play in Education (PIE) Conference, Young Inventor Challenge (YIC), Media Influencer Event and PlayCHIC Fashion Show.
